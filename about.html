📜 History

Tirumangalam is a town in Madurai district, Tamil Nadu. The name comes from making sacred ornaments (Mangalyam) for deities here in ancient times. It has a rich history in culture, trade, and spirituality.

⭐ Famous For

  • ⛏️ Lock and Iron Works
  • 🛣️ National Highway (NH44) Transport Hub
  • 🙏 Mukthi Nilayam & Kamatchi Amman Temples
  • 🕌 Mosques and Churches showing faith diversity
  • 🎉 Festivals celebrated with unity

🏙️ Modern Tirumangalam

Today, Tirumangalam is a growing town with schools, colleges, industries, and markets. It plays a key role as a commercial and cultural hub in Madurai district.

📍 Location

Tirumangalam is located just 20 km southwest of Madurai city, along the busy NH44 highway. Its strategic location makes it well-connected for travel and trade across Tamil Nadu.

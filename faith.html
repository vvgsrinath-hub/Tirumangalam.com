Places of Worship

Tirumangalam is home to people of different faiths. Here are some important mosques and churches in the town:

Jamia Masjid

Located in the heart of Tirumangalam, Jamia Masjid is one of the prominent mosques where the Muslim community gathers for daily prayers and special festivals like Eid.

St. Mary’s Church

St. Mary’s Church serves the Christian community of Tirumangalam. It hosts Sunday prayers, Christmas celebrations, and community gatherings.

Unity in Diversity

The people of Tirumangalam live in harmony, celebrating festivals of all religions with joy and togetherness. Faith centres here symbolize peace, respect, and cultural unity.

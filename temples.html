Sathya Yuga Srishti Temple (Mukthi Nilayam)

Location: Rayapalayam Village Road, East Street, Melakkottai, Tamil Nadu – 625706

Contact: +91 94430 32619 | satyaamirth1938@gmail.com

Timings: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Mukthi Nilayam, also called Sathya Yuga Srishti Temple, is a unique spiritual complex housing 127 mini-temples and over 500 statues of Hindu deities, Jesus, Buddha, and the Navagrahas (astrological planets). Entry is free, while pooja fees apply.

This local shrine draws devotees for its serene atmosphere and traditional worship of Lord Perumal.

Sri Meenakshi Amman Temple

Location: SH-73, Tirumangalam, Tamil Nadu – 625706

A sacred shrine dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi Amman, revered as the divine mother. Devotees believe that worshipping here brings prosperity, good health, and blessings for family harmony.

Major Festivals: Navaratri, Aadi Pooram, and the annual temple festival.

Sri Pathrakali Mariamman Temple

Location: Venkatasamuthiram Geramam, Jaihindpuram, Tirumangalam, Tamil Nadu – 625706

A sacred shrine dedicated to Goddess Pathrakali Mariamman, revered for blessings of prosperity, health, and family well-being.

Major Festival: Vaigasi Mahorchavam, celebrated with grandeur attracting devotees from surrounding regions.

Arulmigu Subramaniya Swamy Temple

Location: 146a, Periya Ratha Veethi, Thiruparankundram, Madurai – 625005

Deity: Lord Murugan and Goddess Deivayanai

Significance: One of the six sacred abodes of Lord Murugan, believed to be the site of his marriage to Goddess Deivayanai.

Timings: Open daily from 5:00 AM to 1:00 PM and from 4:00 PM to 8:30 PM

Festivals: Vaikasi Visakam, Avani Festival, Skanda Sasti Festival, Masi Festival
